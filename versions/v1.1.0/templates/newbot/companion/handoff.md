> The message each session leaves for the next. Where the QUEUE says what is active and STATUS says where the user is at, HANDOFF says what just happened in the last conversation - what was said, what landed, what is left hanging. For a companion archetype, the handoff often carries emotional state and conversational threads that do not fit in a checklist. Newest block at the top. Older blocks trim out to `{DOMAIN}_LOG.md`.
